Does Orbitcheck work with my ecommerce platform?

Yes! We provide native integrations for Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Magento. For custom platforms, our API-first approach means you can integrate with any system. We keep integrations lightweight so you maintain control of your billing and roadmap.

How fast is the validation?

Lightning fast. Our p95 response time is under 150ms, with cached responses returning in under 50ms. We use edge locations and intelligent caching to ensure validation never slows down your checkout.

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II compliant and GDPR ready. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We offer data residency options for enterprise customers and provide a comprehensive DPA upon request.
